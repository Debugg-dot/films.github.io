About


It all started with a Problem, while I was exploring Filmmaking

The journey of filmmaking involves lots of study and learning. Much goes into the experimenting and creative part. This website (which is inspired by freeCodeCamp) is the result of the experiences and hurdles I faced in my journey.

The Problem: Large no. of unorganised resources

The learning curve is a bit difficult. There are tons of unorganised resources out there for the filmmakers' utility. Many find it overwhelming to have so many good resources scattered all over the internet in this manner. The right assets for the right projects are difficult to find.

The Solution: All free filmmaking stuff, compiled at one place!

One can find all assets, resources and softwares available on different websites at one single place. There is no hasel of saving websites and bookmarks. This gives all beginners a platform with all resources required when geting started.
